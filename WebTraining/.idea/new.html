What is this page about?

On this page I will tell you about my preferences in the holidays.I have pretty basic preferences but still I will write about them.
Right now i'm working and not on holiday, but maybe I can get some relaxtion through creating this page and thinking back to those days.

My interests in the holiday

Going to the beach

    I adore to go to the beach and to swim in the sea.
    I also like to play with the sand or just to lay in a
    lounger. The beach is an absoloute paradise to me. I almost
    like everything on the beach, except the insects, jellyfish and
    so on. Nevertheless to go to the beach always relaxed me more than something else.

Shopping

    I LOVE to go shopping no matter where I am.
    Shopping makes me so happy. To buy something
    for me or for someone else is a great feeling
    to me. I especially like to buy stuffs for people
    I really like. That makes me and them happy at
    the same time. So for me shopping is a must in
    holidays.

Visiting special places

    In my opinion it is also a must to me to visit special
    places in the holidays. For example if you're in paris
    it would make sense to visit the eiffel tower. I mean
    if you're already there and have the opportunity then
    why not. But I also love to visit leisure parks. I
    always have fun there.

Summary

All in all the main thing you should do in the holiday is to recover yourself from the hard times. Enjoy your next holidays and have a good time.
